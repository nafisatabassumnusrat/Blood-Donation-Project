About This Project

This project — Blood Donor Finder Bangladesh — is a frontend prototype designed to demonstrate how web technology can solve real-life social problems.

In Bangladesh, blood emergencies are a daily reality. Thousands of lives are at risk due to difficulty in locating compatible donors quickly. This platform bridges the gap between donors and those in urgent need.

Donor data is stored securely using browser localStorage — meaning your information stays on your device. No data is shared with third parties.

Health Tips

Blood Donation Awareness

Everything you need to know about donating blood safely

❤️
Blood Donation Saves Lives

One unit of blood can save up to 3 lives. Accident victims, surgery patients, and cancer patients all depend on voluntary donors.

📅
Donate Every 3 Months

Healthy adults can safely donate whole blood every 3 months (90 days). Your body replenishes blood within 4–8 weeks.

💧
Drink Plenty of Water

Stay well-hydrated before donation. Drink at least 500ml of water in the hours before your appointment for a smoother process.

🥗
Eat Healthy Beforehand

Have a nutritious, iron-rich meal 2–3 hours before donating. Avoid fatty foods. Eat well after donation to replenish energy.

⚖️
Weight & Age Eligibility

Donors must be 18–60 years old and weigh at least 50kg. You'll pass a brief health check before donating.

😴
Rest After Donation

Take it easy for 24 hours after donating. Avoid heavy exercise, smoking, and alcohol on donation day.

🩺
Blood Is Tested For Safety

Every donated unit is tested for infectious diseases before use. Blood banks follow strict protocols to ensure patient safety.

🌟
Be a Voluntary Donor

Voluntary, unpaid donors are the safest source of blood. Registering as a regular donor helps build a reliable supply for emergencies.

— 01 — DATA STORAGE

Data Storage

Blood Donor Finder Bangladesh stores all donor information exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This means your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external server or third party.

🔒 Your data stays 100% on your own device — always private and secure.
— 02 — INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Information We Collect

When you register as a donor, we collect only what is necessary to connect you with those in need:

  • Full Name — to identify you as a donor
  • Blood Group — to match with recipients
  • Location / Area — to show nearby donors
  • Phone Number — so recipients can contact you directly
  • Last Donation Date (optional) — to indicate donation readiness
— 03 — WHAT WE DON'T DO

What We Don't Do

  • We do not share your data with any third parties or advertisers
  • We do not use cookies for tracking purposes
  • We do not collect analytics or behavioral data
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers
— 04 — DATA DELETION

Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage via your browser's privacy or data settings. Since data is stored only locally, there is no server-side data to request deletion of.

— 05 — YOUR CONSENT

Your Consent

By registering as a donor on this platform, you consent to your information being stored in your browser's localStorage and being visible to other users of the same browser session who search for donors.

— 06 — CONTACT

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the developer's GitHub or LinkedIn profile linked in the footer.
